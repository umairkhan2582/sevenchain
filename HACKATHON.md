  ## Why Build on Seven Chain?

  - **Zero trading fees** — your protocol keeps 100% of value flow
  - **Live mainnet** — real users, real volume, real bridge (not a testnet)
  - **2001× perp futures infrastructure** — hook into it, extend it, build on top
  - **Instant faucet** — 0.1 SEVEN to your wallet in seconds, no sign-up
  - **Hardhat + Foundry ready** — deploy in under 5 minutes

  ## Themes

  We're looking for dApps in three categories:

  ### 🪙 DeFi & Trading
  AMMs, lending protocols, yield strategies, perp aggregators, copy-trading bots, liquidation bots, on-chain order books.

  ### 🚀 Meme & Social
  Token launchpads, social trading, meme NFT collections, on-chain leaderboards, prediction markets, community vaults.

  ### 🌉 Infrastructure
  Bridge tools, explorer plugins, wallet connectors, RPC gateways, analytics dashboards, dev tooling, indexers.
